When Fire Meets Mist

This work explores the raw force of energy as both a natural and emotional phenomenon. The lightning at the center is not meant to be a literal depiction of a storm, but a visual metaphor for power breaking through resistance. Its sharp, jagged veins cut across the canvas, creating tension and movement, while the surrounding clouds soften and frame the chaos.

Created by: Jerome Boyd Jr.

The Last Light

This piece captures the battle between creation and destruction, where purity (the glowing tree) resists being consumed by chaos. Surrounded by a sea of red that evokes violence and raw emotion, and threatened by black drips of corruption, the tree stands as a luminous symbol of life and resilience. The faint, watchful eyes suggest judgment or fate, bearing witness to the struggle, yet the tree endures, glowing defiantly against destruction.

Created by Jerome Boyd Jr.

Through Smoke and Ash

This piece reflects resilience in the face of chaos. The blue silhouette, running through fire and smoke, symbolizes defiance and hope against destruction. Surrounded by shifting, unpredictable forces, the figure moves forward — embodying the human struggle to escape turmoil and pursue transformation.

Created by: Jerome Boyd Jr.

About me

My name is Jerome Boyd, and I’m a 34-year-old graphic designer who transitioned into full-stack development. With a background in design, I bring a strong eye for aesthetics and detail to every project, ensuring that what I create is not only functional but also visually compelling. Over time, I discovered a passion for development that allows me to merge creativity with problem-solving, building digital experiences that are both user-friendly and engaging. I enjoy tackling challenges, learning new technologies, and pushing ideas from concept to polished reality. Whether it’s designing sleek visuals, developing robust applications, or blending both worlds, I’m driven by the goal of making things that look beautiful and work seamlessly.
